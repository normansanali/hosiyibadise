Scantily-Clad Megan Fox Flirts With Brian Austin Green in Wedding Band

Megan Fox and Brian Austin Green fell in love on the set of ABC’s Hope and Faith eight years ago, and now the spouses of two years have teamed up once again on the small screen.

Fox guest stars on the Nov. 17 episode of her husband’s new TBS series, Wedding Band, and TV Guide has an exclusive clip. (The episode was presumably taped before she gave birth to son Noah Sept. 27.)

The 26-year-old plays an actress named Alexa who stars in a hit sci-fi series, Plutopia. Wearing a sexy, midriff-baring ensemble made of “genuine Italian space leather,” Alexa meets Tommy (Green) after his band books a gig at Comic-Con.
