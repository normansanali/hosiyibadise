Laslo Djere Girlfriend Tamara Pavicevic Is A Makeup Artist

Laslo Djere’s girlfriend, Tamara Pavicevic, is an Instagram influencer and makeup artist from Serbia. Laslo and Tamara have been together since 2018.

The Serbian tennis player can give his girlfriend the best birthday present today as he takes on Alexander Zverev in the German Open final. The makeup artist turned 31 on July 29. 

Since beginning to date in 2018, the couple has been seen several times on each other’s social media pages, including Tamara’s vlogs. 

Since turning professional in 2013, Laslo has won two ATP titles, with the first one being in 2019 against Dominic Thiem in Rio Open. His second ATP title came in 2020 at the Fort Village Sardegna Open. 

Now in German Open, he can win his third title against the favorite Alexander Zverev, who can potentially win his 20th ATP title. 

Laslo Djere Girlfriend, Tamara Pavicevic

Laslo Djere’s girlfriend, Tamara Pavicevic, is a makeup artist based in Serbia. She launched her Instagram makeup page earlier this year in April. 

The better half of tennis player is quite active on social media and has gained 23.4k followers on Instagram. She also has a YouTube channel where she uploads vlogs and makeup tutorials.

Tamara started uploading makeup videos on YouTube seven years ago when it was one of the biggest trends but wasn’t consistent.

She has gained 35.6k subscribers on YouTube and in recent months has started sharing clips from her travel to various tennis games to cheer on her beau. 

But makeup isn’t the only skill Tamara can pull off from her hands. She is also a brilliant cook and is the creator of Cooking With Sonja. The Instagram page of Cooking With Sonja has 2k followers since its launch in 2020.

From baking to cooking, there isn’t any dish that Tamara can’t make. She has previously shared recipes for her Fresh Strawberry Cake and Tacos Salad on her Instagram page. Tamara has also shared a YouTube video of preparing the salad. 

Laslo Djere And Girlfriend Tamara Pavicevic Relationship

Earlier this year, on Valentine’s, Tamara celebrated her relationship with the tennis player by sharing a sweet video of them throughout several years.

In the caption of the shared video, Tamara hinted they had been together for more than four years. It checks out as the couple shared their first picture at the end of 2018. 

Tamara was the first to go public with their relationship sharing a loved-up picture with Laslo on December 15, 2018, in Žabljak. On Christmas, Tamara shared another picture with the tennis player placing a kiss on her lips.

After celebrating Christmas in their homeland, Tamara traveled to India, as the Serbian competed in the TATA Open Maharashtra. Laslo first shared the picture of Tamara on his Instagram on Valentine’s Day 2019. 

Since 2019, Tamara also began showing her tennis beau on her vlog videos, with one of their first being the trip to Morocco. 2019 was also the year Laslo won his first ATP title and broke into the top 30. 

The couple capped off a fine 2019 with a trip to UAE as Tamara shared a gallery of images from their adventures.

With Laslo’s job giving him the luxury to travel around the globe, the tennis player has made several memories with his girlfriend in some of the most exquisite locations. 

On June 2, the couple celebrated Laslo’s 28th birthday with a homemade cake by Tamara. And Tamara will be hoping to receive her special birthday present with her beau beating Alexander Zverev in the German Open final.
