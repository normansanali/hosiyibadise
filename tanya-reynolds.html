Tanya Reynolds is an English actress who is best known for having been cast as Teresa Benelli in Delicious and as Lily in S*x Education. Apart from that, she has also been cast in other shows and movies like Fanny Lye Deliver’d, Lily Meets Charlie, Emma., Death in Paradise, Outlander, The Mallorca Files, and For Love or Money.
