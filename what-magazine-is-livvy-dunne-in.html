What magazine is Livvy Dunne in?

Olivia Dunne, the talented LSU gymnast, is set to make her debut in the 2023 Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue. This announcement was made by Sports Illustrated, and the magazine has shared early images of Dunne. Despite missing most of the season due to injury, she has made a remarkable return to the Tigers gymnastics team. Known for her significant social media presence, Dunne is ranked as the NCAA's second-highest earning female athlete in terms of name, image, and likeness valuation. Her feature in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue is highly anticipated and is scheduled for release on May 12th.

Additionally, Inside Gymnastics Magazine has recognized Olivia Dunne's remarkable talent by naming her to their list of the 50 Most Photogenic athletes. The article about Dunne highlights her journey through the junior levels, her transition to a senior competitor, and her impressive performances at the U.S. Classic and U.S. Championships in 2018. It emphasizes her strong presence, quality of dance elements, and creativity in routines, as well as her aspirations to represent the USA internationally and at the Olympics. The magazine expressed excitement in following her progress as she continues to set new standards for ambitious artistry and photogenic floor routines.
