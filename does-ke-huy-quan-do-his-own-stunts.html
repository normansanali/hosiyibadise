Does Ke Huy Quan do his own stunts?

Ke Huy Quan also known as Jonathan Ke Quan, is a Vietnamese-American actor and stunt choreographer.

Quan is best known for playing Short Round in Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom (1984) and Data in The Goonies (1985).

Between 1990 and 1991, he co-starred as Jasper Kwong in the sitcom Head of the Class over two seasons.

Quan stopped acting due to a lack of opportunity in the late 1990s, when he received his film degree from USC School of Cinematic Arts, after which he went on to work as a stunt coordinator and assistant director.

Does Ke Huy Quan do his own stunts?

In 2022, Quan starred as Waymond Wang in the film Everything Everywhere All at Once and according to the Insider, he did most of his own stunts.

“Everything Everywhere” drew inspiration from classic martial-arts movies, which routinely showcase fights with fewer cuts and camera moves compared to modern Hollywood action movies.
