Ashley Scott Wiki Biography

Ashley Scott was born on the 13th July 1977, in Metairie, Louisiana USA, and is an actress and model, perhaps best known from her roles in such television series as “Dark Angel” (2001-2002), “Birds of Prey” (2002-2003), and “Jericho” (2006-2008). Scott has also played is such movies as “A.I. Artificial Intelligence” (2001), “Walking Tall” (2004), and “Into the Blue” (2005). Her career started in 2001.

Have you ever wondered how rich Ashley Scott is as of early 2017? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that Scott’s net worth is as high as $500,000, an amount earned largely through her acting career, but in addition to her work on film and television, Scott has also worked as a model, which has improved her wealth somewhat.

Ashley Scott Net Worth $500,000

Ashley Scott grew up in Charleston, South Carolina, and became a model as a young girl, while in 1993 she was an Elite Model Look finalist. Soon after, Scott started working for Elite Miami, and modelled internationally on the catwalk at various fashion shows in such cities as Miami, Paris and London among others. Her net worth was already well set.

In 2001, Ashley debuted as an actress in Steven Spielberg’s Oscar Award-nominated “A.I. Artificial Intelligence” starring Haley Joel Osment, Jude Law and Frances O’Connor, while in the same year she started playing in the Golden Globe Award-nominated series “Dark Angel” with Jessica Alba. Scott appeared in 21 episodes of the show until 2002, and then starred in the series called “Birds of Prey”, from 2002 to 2003, both of which contributed steadily to her net worth.

Ashley continued with roles in such movies as “S.W.A.T.” (2003) alongside Samuel L. Jackson, Colin Farrell and Michelle Rodriguez, “Walking Tall” (2004) starring Dwayne Johnson, and “Lost” (2004) with Dean Cain and Danny Trejo. In the mid-2000s, Ashley played in “Into the Blue” (2005) alongside Paul Walker, Jessica Alba and Scott Caan, “Just Friends” (2005) with Ryan Reynolds and Amy Smart, and in “Puff, Puff, Pass” (2006) starring Ronnie Warner, Danny Masterson, and Mekhi Phifer, so her net worth continued to rise.

From 2006 to 2008, Scott played Emily Sullivan in 29 episodes of the Primetime Emmy Award-nominated series “Jericho”, while she also appeared is such films as “The Kingdom” (2007) with Jamie Foxx, Chris Cooper and Jennifer Garner, “Strange Wilderness” (2008), and “12 Rounds” (2009) starring John Cena. In 2013, Scott starred in “The Nightmare Nanny”, while in 2015, she appeared in seven episodes of the Primetime Emmy Award-nominated “UnREAL”. Most recently, Scott worked in “16 and Missing” (2016) and “Broken Promise” (2016), while at the moment she is filming “A Woman Deceived”, which will be released by the end of 2017.

Regarding her personal life, Ashley Scott firstly married producer Anthony Rhulen in 2004, but they divorced in 2008. She subsequently married musician Steve Hart in 2010, and has two daughters with him. Scott currently lives in Los Angeles, California.
