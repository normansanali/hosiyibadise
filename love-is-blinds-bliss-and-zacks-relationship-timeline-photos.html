Love Is Blind's Bliss Poureetezadi and Zack Goytowski's Relationship Timeline: Pod Love Triangle to Newlyweds

Love is blind — and worth a second chance — for season 4 couple Zack Goytowski and Bliss Poureetezadi.

The attorney sparked immediate bonds with Bliss and Irina Solomonova in the Love Is Blind pods, which aired in March 2023. Despite Bliss declaring her interest and baking him a surprise birthday treat, Zack chose to propose sight unseen to Irina.

Bliss left the Seattle-set experiment heartbroken as Zack and the Solo owner pursued their relationship in person. They pulled the plug on their engagement after less than one week together.

“I would say I made a mistake with not picking Bliss [in the first place],” Zack exclusively told Us Weekly in March 2023. “I knew that when I ended it [with Irina] and was kind of dealing with that. I don’t know how much you could see of the turmoil that I was going through, but when I did end it, then my very first thing was I wanted to go and tell [producers] to bring [Bliss] back and that I had made the wrong choice, but I knew I had to live with the decision.”

When Zack returned home from the cast’s Mexico trip, he reunited with Bliss and proposed. They wed in May 2022.
