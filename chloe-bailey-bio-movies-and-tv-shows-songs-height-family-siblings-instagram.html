Chloe Bailey Bio, Movies And TV Shows, Songs, Height, Family, Siblings, Instagram

Chloe Elizabeth Bailey, known by stage name Chlöe, is an American singer, songwriter, and actress.

She was born on July 1, 1998 in Atlanta, Georgia, United States.

Chloe is Known for being one half of the musical duo Chloe x Halle with her sister Halle Bailey. Together they have earned five Grammy Award nominations since 2018.

In 2021, she debuted as a solo artist with “Have Mercy”.

In November 2022, she sang the national anthem during Game 3 at the 2022 World Series.

On January 24, 2023, Chloe announced her debut solo album, titled In Pieces, with a release date set for March 2023.

Chloe Bailey Movies And TV Shows

Chloe Bailey has starred in several movies and tv shows including Choreographers React (2022), Grown-ish (2018–2022), Jane (2022), Why the Sun and the Moon Live in the Sky (2021), Black-ish (2020), Austin & Ally (2013), Let It Shine (2012), Meet the Browns (2008), Last Holiday (2006), and many others.

Chloe Bailey Songs

Chloe Bailey has a couple of songs to her credits including Have Mercy, Treat Me and Pray It Away.

Chloe Bailey Height

Chloe Bailey stands at a height of 5 feet 5 inches (1.65 m) tall.

Chloe Bailey Family

Chloe Bailey is the daughter of Courtney Bailey and Doug Bailey.

Chloe Bailey Siblings

Chloe Bailey has three siblings; Halle Bailey, Branson Bailey and Ski Bailey.

Chloe Bailey Instagram
