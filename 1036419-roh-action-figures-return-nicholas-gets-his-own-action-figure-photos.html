ROH Action Figures Return, Nicholas Gets His Own Action Figure (Photos)

Figures Toy Company and WrestlingSuperStore.com revealed a shocking surprise yesterday that created a huge buzz amongst wrestling figure collectors.

ROH Action Figures Return

Figures Toy Co. broke the wrestling figure community last night with the huge announcement that their Ring of Honor action figures would not only be returning, but new and old figures would be available immediately on the Wrestling SuperStore website. New faces such as Dalton Castle, Raymond Rowe and Roderick Strong surfaced amongst many others! Be sure to get them before they’re gone as there’s no word on how long they will be sticking around for again.

Nicholas Gets His Own Action Figure

One half of the RAW tag team champions, Nicholas, received his very own custom action figure from Figures Toy Company, which is pretty awesome! He has a Mattel WWE RAW Tag Title as well as a custom figure shirt made by Thread Head Custom Clothing. Definitely a one of a kind figure!
