Does Jack Dylan Grazer Have a Girlfriend? He Doesn't Seem to Be Dating Anyone

Upcoming star Jack Dylan Grazer seems not to have a girlfriend at the moment. With his history of PDA, his lack of recent posts about his romantic adventures suggest that he is single.

Grazer is one of Hollywood's most exciting young talents and already has multiple blockbuster films under his belt. Coupled with a charming character and good looks, the young showman is not short of admirers.

Grazer, who revealed that he is bisexual in 2021, has had an increased interest in his love life from fans as his profile as a Hollywood actor has risen in leaps and bounds over the years.

Jack Previously Dated Tiktoker Morgan Cohen, but It Seems They're Not Together Anymore

In March 2022, speculation began swirling around that Grazer was dating Cohen after the TikToker shared a suggestive video of the two lip-syncing on the streaming platform. It didn't take long for fans to discover the truth as the pair laid it bare right in front of their eyes for everyone to see.

It is safe to say that the couple did not attempt to hide their romance, as Cohen would refer to Grazer as her boyfriend on social media and posted many pictures of the pair kissing on her Instagram. The couple also engaged in some PDA in front of photographers at red-carpet events.

For example, while attending the Venice Film Festival in 2022, Grazer and Cohen kissed for the cameras as they walked on the red carpet. They repeated the act as they arrived for the "My Policeman" premiere in Los Angeles on November 1, 2022.

However, their relationship may have hit a bump as the photos of the pair together have since dried up. Although nothing has been confirmed, their lack of public appearances together is perhaps a sign of trouble in paradise and, worse, a breakup.
