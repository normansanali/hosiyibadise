Danica McKellar Engaged to Scott Sveslosky: See a Picture of Her Ring

Danica McKellar‘s Wonder Years may still be ahead of her! The Dancing With the Stars alum is engaged to marry boyfriend Scott Sveslosky, she revealed via Twitter and Facebook on Wednesday, July 16. 

“So…can you guess what my big announcement is?” she captioned a photo of herself smiling and pointing to a large diamond ring on her left hand. “Yes, my amazing boyfriend Scott and I are engaged!” 

“This is the guy that Valentin Chmerkovskiy did a shout-out to on Dancing With the Stars!” she added, referring to the season 18 episode when Chmerkovskiy thanked McKellar’s “amazing boyfriend Scott” for being so cool about letting him dance with the actress.

“Hugs to you all!” she tweeted at the end of her announcement. On Facebook, she added: “I’m wishing all of you the happiness and joy that I feel right now!”
