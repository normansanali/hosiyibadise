Sherri Saum Reveals First Look At Twin Boys — Pics Below

Who wants to see a cute pair of newborn hands? Duh, of course you do!

On May 14, Sherri welcomed her twin boys and announced the happy news on Twitter. She even gave them their own hashtag!

“The twitter-patter of four new feet. Welcome to the world John Ruben and Michael Luis! #doubledelosreyes,” read her first tweet about her sons.

John and Michael weighed 5 lbs, 7 oz and 5 lbs, 15 oz, respectively, reports the Daily Mail.

Now, Sherri and her husband have revealed the first pictures of their precious baby boys — and yes, the pictures are just stunning!

CLICK TO SEE: Sherri Reveals First Pictures Of Twin Boys

One photo shows the happy couple posing in bed, each of them holding on their newborn babies. Another shows the twins curled up next to each other in an oversized wooden bowl. So cute!

Sherri Says Breastfeeding Is Just Like Breathing

Along with the first photos of her twins, Sherri gave an interview discussing how motherhood has changed her life forever.

One of the most important things she discussed was how breastfeeding really surprised her because she thought it would freak her out.

“[…] breastfeeding has become like breathing. I used to think I would be freaked out when I’m out in public, but Kamar and I take the boys with us to dinner, and I just throw on my little cape thingy, and I don’t miss a beat,” Sherri shared.

Good for you, Sherri!
