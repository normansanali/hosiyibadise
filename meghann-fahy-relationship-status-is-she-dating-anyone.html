Meghann Fahy Relationship Status: Is She Dating Anyone?

Meghann Fahy joined HBO’s The White Lotus for its second season, and her character, Daphne Sullivan, was married to Cameron Sullivan (played by Theo James).

But is Meghann married in real life? What’s her relationship status? And who is she dating? Find answers below.

Meghann Fahy Relationship Status: Is She Dating Anyone?

It’s unclear if Meghann Fahy is in a relationship now (as of 2022), but we do know that she was last dating the actor Billy Magnussen.

The two first met back in Jan 2011 on the set of their Hallmark flick The Lost Valentine — Meghann played the younger version of Betty White’s character “Caroline Robinson Thomas” while Billy starred as “Neil Thomas.”

However, they didn’t start dating until 2017.

Thereafter, the two started sharing posts about each other on their respective IGs which suddenly stopped in May 2020. Yes, sadly, the last picture Billy had with Meghann on his Instagram was in that month, which suggests that they may have split.

Just 2 months before the post, the two also made their last red-carpet appearance together.

As for those of you wondering if something is up between Meghann and her on-screen romance Theo James, the two are just professional actors. Even on the show, the two might be married and depicted as they are crazy in love with each other, but doesn’t last long.

Meghann’s ex-boyfriend, Billy Magnussen is an American actor who’s graced movies like Ingrid Goes West, Game Night, the Aladdin reboot, and No Time to Die, as well as shows like Made for Love and Get Shorty.

He is 5 years older than Meghann.

Growing up as a jock in Georgia, Billy started his acting career accidentally. Infact, he was into wrestling, and one day after taking high school theater to fulfill his gym requirement, he realized how much he enjoyed the craft and eventually decided to pursue it post–high school.

Fast forward to over a decade later, he was finally an established actor who was also rumored to be gay.

One reason people believed him to be gay was that he made his theatrical debut through Terrence McNally’s Broadway show, The Ritz, in 2007. The plot of the drama was set in a gay bathhouse based in Manhattan. You can connect the dots now.

Also, before 2017, Terrence hardly shared his personal life in the media. So,  netizens assumed that he was hiding in the closet.

Meghann Fahy Movies And TV Shows

Meghann Fahy started shaping her career ever since she was 8. At the time, she was publicly singing, and as she grew up, she did her senior class plays. Also, during her senior in high school, her mom asked her to audition for an open call in New York.

Surprisingly, instead of what they expected (a musical), the audition was a  Spider-Man. A month after she sang for the directors, Meghann then received a call to come and audition for the theater, Next to Normal. This would be her first-ever professional job.

Thereafter, Meghann moved to D.C. for three months, and then when she came back, the show was already moving to Broadway. So, they rehearsed in March of 2009, and then she moved to New York where she stayed with another understudy friend for a couple of more weeks.

The same year, Meghann also landed a role in Gossip Girl as “Devyn”. Thus, her TV career began.

So far, Meghann has appeared in TV shows like The Good Wife, Georgetown, One Life to Live, Necessary Roughness, Chicago Fire, It Could Be Worse, Law & Order, The Bold Type, and more.

Also, she’s graced movies like The Lost Valentine, Those People, Burning Bodhi, Our Time, Miss Sloane, The Bracket Theory, W4m, and more.

Meghann Fahy Weight Loss

Meghann Fahy wasn’t always this fit. She’s been through a weight loss program of her own and has maintained her body ever since.

Her routine includes — drinking a lot of water, working out (running, breathing exercises, and meditating), eating eggs every day, and using eye patches. Yes, the star was super into eye patches. “I use Skyn, which doesn’t slide or rip. Also, I was introduced to the jade roller. [I] put it in the freezer and roll it around [my] face,” she explained.
