Kelly Doran: Prolific Real Estate Developer Dies at 66

Remembering Kelly Doran: A Visionary Real Estate Developer’s Legacy

The real estate community in Minneapolis/St. Paul mourns the loss of Kelly Doran, a prolific and visionary real estate developer who left an indelible mark on the landscape of the Twin Cities.

On December 28, 2023, Kelly Doran passed away at the age of 66, leaving behind a legacy of transformative projects and a commitment to excellence in the field of real estate development.

A Visionary in Real Estate

Kelly Doran was widely recognized as a visionary in the field of real estate development. Throughout his career, he demonstrated an unparalleled commitment to reshaping the Twin Cities through innovative and transformative projects.

Notable Multifamily Housing Developments

One of Kelly Doran’s most significant contributions was his involvement in some of the Twin Cities’ most notable multifamily housing developments.

His projects helped redefine neighborhoods and contributed to the vibrancy of the community. Doran’s dedication to creating high-quality, sustainable living spaces had a lasting impact on the region’s real estate landscape.

Reshaping the Neighborhood

Kelly Doran’s influence extended beyond the blueprints of his developments. His commitment to community-building and neighborhood revitalization played a pivotal role in reshaping the urban fabric.

Behind Thousands of Apartment Units

As a real estate developer, Kelly Doran was the driving force behind the creation of thousands of apartment units across the Twin Cities.

Courageous Battle with Cancer

Kelly Doran faced his final challenge with the same courage and determination that defined his professional career.

Following a courageous battle with cancer, he passed away, leaving behind a legacy that will be remembered by colleagues, residents, and the communities he touched.
