Giovanni Ribisi career earnings, salary and net worth

Antonino Giovanni Ribisi (born December 17, 1974) is an American actor best known for his roles in the TV series Sneaky Pete and the films Avatar (2009), Lost in Translation (2003), Ted (2012) and its sequel Ted 2 (2015), Contraband (2012), and others.

What is Giovanni Ribisi  net worth?

Giovanni Ribisi has a net worth of $20 million.

How much does Giovanni Ribisi make a year?

Giovanni Ribisi earns $3 million annually.

Ribisi began acting as a child, appearing in the 1980s sitcom My Two Dads and in films in the 1990s. Ribisi was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Guest Actor in a Comedy Series for his work on My Name Is Earl. Ribisi appeared in James Cameron’s film Avatar.

Ribisi directed the music video for “Siberian Nights,” the third single from The Kills’ 2016 album Ash & Ice. In the Amazon series Sneaky Pete, Ribisi portrayed Marius. Ribisi married actress Mariah O’Brien in 1997, and they have a daughter together. In 2001, the couple divorced. Ribisi began dating indie rock singer-songwriter Cat Power in 2007.
