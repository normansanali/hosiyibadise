Juanita Moore Net Worth

Juanita Moore was an American actress born on October 19, 1914 in Mississippi. She is best known for her Oscar-nominated performance as Annie Johnson in the 1959 film Imitation of Life. Moore's performance was praised by critics and earned her a nomination for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress.
