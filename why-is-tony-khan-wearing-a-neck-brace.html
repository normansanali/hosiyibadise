Why is Tony Khan wearing a neck brace?

Tony Khan, the owner of All Elite Wrestling (AEW) and the chief football strategy officer of the Jacksonville Jaguars, has been seen wearing a neck brace during the 2024 NFL Draft. This unexpected attire has sparked curiosity and raised questions about the reason behind it.

AEW Wrestling Incident

The primary reason behind Tony Khan's neck brace is an incident that occurred during a broadcast of AEW Dynamite.

Tony Khan, who is actively involved in AEW as the CEO and founder, was involved in a scripted altercation within the wrestling ring. During the show, he was subjected to a wrestling move known as a piledriver and took a punch to the gut, leading to a dramatic turn of events.

AEW and NFL Synergy

The decision to wear the neck brace during the NFL Draft was not just a coincidence. Tony Khan's appearance in the Jaguars' draft war room with the neck brace served as a unique marketing opportunity for AEW.

It created a synergy between the worlds of professional wrestling and American football, drawing attention to both AEW and the NFL Draft.

Honoring Wrestling Storyline

The decision to continue wearing the neck brace during the NFL Draft was also attributed to honoring the wrestling storyline.

Many speculated whether Khan's choice to wear the neck brace was a way of selling the effects of the scripted attack and promoting AEW on a massive stage like the NFL Draft.

Tony Khan's presence in the NFL Draft war room, donning the neck brace, did not go unnoticed. It drew reactions from various personalities, including Pat McAfee, who works for WWE, mentioning the storyline on his show.

Also, Khan's statements during the draft interviews, where he promoted AEW and addressed the rivalry with WWE, added an extra layer of drama to the situation.
