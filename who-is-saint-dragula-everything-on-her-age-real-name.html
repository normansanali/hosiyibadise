Who Is Saint Dragula? Everything On Her Age &amp; Real Name

Saint Dragula has proved her powers as a true drag icon by competing in and winning The Boulet Brothers’ Dragula: Resurrectio Halloween special in 2020, and now she has returned as a Runway Demon Queen of Season 4.

Saint Dragula is the first Dragula contestant to compete in the third season.

On October 6, 2021, Saint was announced as a contestant on The Boulet Brothers’ Dragula Season 4.

She has won a cash prize of $20,000 as the winner of Resurrection and a spot on Dragula’s fourth season.

She has been doing drag for five years as of 2021.

She has participated in the Vampire Look, where she got eliminated because of her theme.

Who Is Saint Dragula?

Saint Dragula is a drag queen and a participant of The Boulet Brothers’ Dragula.

She was formally known as St. Lucia is one of the eleven contestants to compete on the third season of The Boulet Brothers’ Dragula.

Saint says her name originates from the book American Gods by Neil Gaiman from the Character Saint Lucia.

The Boulet Brothers’ Dragula is an international competition to uncover the world’s next Drag Supermonster. The reality show is based on their club event and nightlife pageant.

Saint Dragula Age & Real Name

Saint Dragula’s age is 25 as of 2021.

She was born on December 11, 1995, in Harlem in Manhattan, N.Y.

Saint grew up in Lansing, Michigan.

She is living in Brooklyn, New York, USA.

Her real name is Shaquile Foreman.

Saint dropped out of school when she was fourteen because she needed to work full time.

Saint Dragula Sexuality

Saint Dragula pronouns herself as a She/Her in drag.

However, she pronouns herself as a He/Him out of drag.

Saint seems to be a Gay as she was in a relationship with her former partner Aiden Zhane.

She holds African-American ethnicity.

Saint Dragula Partner

Saint Dragula’s current partner is still the subject of suspense.

However, she has been in>Aiden Zhane.

Aiden Zhane, her drag sister, and former partner, was a contestant on Season 12 of Drag Race.

She met Aiden when she was in town to do some drag performances.
