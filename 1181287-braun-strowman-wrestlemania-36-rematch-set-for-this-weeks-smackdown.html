Braun Strowman &amp; WrestleMania 36 Rematch Set For This Weeks SmackDown

Less than a week after winning the WWE Universal Championship, Braun Strowman is ready to make his first appearance as champion at this week’s episode of Friday Night SmackDown. According to WWE, Strowman will be on hand at SmackDown, although it’s unclear exactly what the Monster Among Men will be up to.

Also scheduled for this week’s SmackDown is a rematch of one of the best matches of WrestleMania 36, when WWE Women’s Tag Team Champions Alexa Bliss and Nikki Cross will defend their titles against The Kabuki Warriors, the same duo they defeated at WrestleMania. This match will no doubt be a must-watch, as all four superstars are extremely proud, and will be giving it their all in an effort to come out on top.
