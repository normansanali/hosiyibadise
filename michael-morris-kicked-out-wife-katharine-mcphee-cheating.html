Michael Morris Kicked Out by Wife After Katharine McPhee Kissing Photos Surface

There has been a development in the Katharine McPhee-kissing-her-married-director story that will shock absolutely no one.

Michael Morris, who worked with McPhee on their former series, 'Smash,' has been kicked out of the home he shared with his wife and three kids after photos of him kissing the star in public surfaced earlier this week.

Actress Mary McCormack threw her hubby out of their Los Angeles home after he divulged the photos were about to leak online. He had apparently attempted to buy them to keep them from circulating, a source revealed to Page Six.

McCormack is also reportedly seeking to end the marriage with the father of her young children, ages 9, 6 and 2. "They have three daughters, so it's incredibly sad," a source says. "He wants to keep the family together, but as far as Mary is concerned, it's over. There's no going back."

Also seemingly over is McPhee's five-year marriage to Nick Cokas, with the couple separating some six months ago.

It sounds like quite a mess, with McPhee smack dab in the middle of two dissolving marriages.
