Eliza Coupe Divorce: Happy Endings Star, Randall Whittinghill Split

Eliza Coupe's Happy Endings appear to be unraveling both on- and off-screen. Two months after the cancellation of her critically acclaimed ABC sitcom Happy Endings, the actress has been served with divorce papers by husband Randall Whittinghill, TMZ reports.

Citing irreconcilable differences, Whittinghill, a puppeteer and acting coach, is said to have requested spousal support in the documents. Per TMZ, however, he and his wife, 32, are working out the division of property in mediation.

Coupe and Whittinghill have been together for more than 12 years. Speaking about their relationship in an interview with New York Magazine's Vulture last October, she joked that she was with him because of his resemblance to her celebrity crush.
