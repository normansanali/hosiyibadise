What does David Beador do for a living?His girlfriend, affair, net worth

Being married to one of the Real Housewives of Orange County actors meant David Beador was saying goodbye to his comfortable private life. For those who don’t know, Beador is a businessman who rose to fame after his divorce from Shannon Beador from the aforementioned reality show.

David and Shannon had been married for seventeen years before their infamous divorce which brought David into the limelight once again. You might be thinking that “celebrity divorce is almost a norm,” but wait until you find out the circumstances surrounding the split which has been a topic of discussion on various platforms for quite some time now. Here are interesting facts you need to know about David Beador.

David who is of American nationality and North American ethnicity was born on December 6, 1964 to his parents in the United States. Although little is known of his parents, David’s father is a renowned businessman who founded the company Beador Construction with him in 1996.

Career – What David Beador does for a living

He is a popular businessman who works with a well-known construction company, Beador Construction. David started with the company choosing to serve as a helping hand to his father who started the company in 1996. With determination and hard work, the Beadors achieved enviable success and propelled the Californian company to massive heights.

The company is primarily in residential as well as non-residential works, such as the construction of streets, highways and bridges. Reports say that they record sales amounting to $50-100 million per year from which they make a total revenue of between $10-25 million.

Net value

The reality star has an estimated net worth of a whopping $20 million, and much of that estimated amount is credited to her commercial relays. He also won from his appearance in reality TV. In 2011, the business mogul acquired the Newport Beach home for $2.35 million.

Personal life – wife, girlfriend, affair

Along with being a renowned business mogul, Beador was also given the spotlight as the husband of Real Housewives of Orange County’s Shannon Beador. David and Shannon were married in December 2000 in a private wedding ceremony with family and friends in attendance.

Together they had three daughters; their first daughter, Sophia and twins Stella and Adeline. However, things have gradually gone downhill between the once happy couple over the years. In December 2017, after announcing their divorce, David and Shannon separated after concluding the process. As a result of the agreement, Shannon was to receive alimony of $12,000 per month for child support and also $18,000 per month for spousal support.

David Beador left with his new girlfriend Lesley Cook, a DIY blogger. Just three months after his divorce from his ex-wife, David went public with Cook on Valentine’s Day 2018. This was after he was seen publicly with the blogger in January 2018. For the businessman, he doesn’t was just moving on, but for the general public, it wasn’t just about moving on, but the fact that he did it rather too soon and too quickly.. It raised a lot of eyebrows and many speculated that Baedor was having an affair with Lesley Cook while still married to Shannon.

The businessman greeted his fans with liked photos of him with Shannon on Instagram and also wished her a happy Valentine’s Day on February 14, 2018. Cook was quick to respond in the most soothing way noting how her life had become brighter since meeting her. While this got fans excited, some others were unhappy with the syndicate.

Shortly after the two brought their love to the public eye, David’s ex-wife, Shannon, gave her version of the circumstances surrounding their divorce. She revealed it on “Watch What Happens Live With Andy Cohen” in July 2018. She hinted that Cook was the reason for their divorce. According to Shannon, her daughters had met Cook during the divorce process. The new couple are said to have met in a SoulCycle class, but the only thing that’s unclear is when they met.

However, according to reports, the couple had already separated in October 2017 before David and Lesley were seen together.
