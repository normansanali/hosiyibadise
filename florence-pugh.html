The British Independent Film Awards-winning Florence Pugh is earning wide media attention from her breakthrough performance in various movies and TV shows. Pugh has starred in Midsommar, Little Women, Fighting With My Family.

The charming actress Florence was born on January 3, 1996, in Oxford, Oxfordshire, England. She is the daughter of restaurateur Clinton Pugh (father) and dance teacher, dancer, Deborah Pugh (mother). Florence has three siblings a brother Toby Sebastian and sisters Arabella Gibbins & Rafaela "Raffie" Pugh.

Who Is Florence Pugh Dating?

The Midsommer actress Florence is in a romantic relationship with Zach Braff. Like Pugh, Zach is also an actor who is famous for playing a role as J.D. on the medical comedy-drama series Scrubs. Braff is also a director, producer, and screenwriter, by profession.

Zach and Florence who were spotted holding hands together in New York City began their romantic affair back in April 2019. Since the day, Pugh and Braff are sharing a good bond as a perfect lovebird. 

The love partner (Florence & Zach) who prefers to keep their relationship status private has also worked together in the film In the Time it Takes to Get There. In the short film, Braff has served as director and Pugh has played a role as Lucille.

21-Years Of Age Gap

The beautiful couple Florence and Zach are facing the rumors regarding their huge age difference. As of 2020, Florence is 24-years-old and Zach is in his mid-40s. There is a 21-years age difference between them. 

The lovebird is ignoring all their criticism and they are living merrily. Back on December 28, 2019, she shared a picture on her Instagram, where an actor Zach commented with a princess emoji. On the same comment, one of the Instagram users responded saying, "You’re 44 years old." Replying on it Florence said, "and yet he got it.

Speaking about the relationship Florence once said, "I have the right to hang out and be with and go out with anyone I want to. I've always found this part of what people do really bizarre. I'm an actor because I like acting and I don't mind people watching my stuff, but people have no right to educate me on my private life."

Past Affairs

Before starting a romantic affair with an actress Pugh, Zach was linked with several women. He first dated an American actress, singer, songwriter, Mandy Moore for two years from 2004 to 2006.

After the separation with Moore, Braff began his romantic relationship with an actress, model, Taylor Bagley in 2009. Zach and Taylor were living blissfully as a perfect love partner. Everything was going fine; however, in 2014, the couple separated.

On the other hand, there are no more details known about Florence's past affairs. But once speaking on the criticism she faced regarding the age gap with Braff, she said, "I'm 24 and I can't choose who I love. There's a reason why I'm not with someone my age, It hasn't worked."

Net Worth

Florence has not revealed the exact net worth she enjoys. But as per estimation, she owns a total fortune of around $2 million. The primary source of acting for Pugh is from her professional acting career. 

The 5-feet-3-inch tall actress Florence has starred in the movies and TV shows like The Falling, Malevolent, Outlaw King, Marcella, King Lear, The Little Drummer Girl, Leading Lady Parts, Lady Macbeth, and others. For the information, Florence will next star in the Marvel Comics film Black Widow alongside actress Scarlett Johansson.
