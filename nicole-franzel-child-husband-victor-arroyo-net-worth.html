Nicole Franzel is an American TV celebrity star who is well-known for her portrayal of roles in The Bold and the Beautiful (1987), The Amazing Race (2001), and The Revengers (2017). After appearing on the Big Brother 16 and 18 show in 2014 and 2016, she rose to fame, respectively.

When Nicole participated in the 2016 show, she became the first person in Big Brother's history to win the Household event. During the course of the 18 show, she won the event, finishing in the first place. She returned to Big Brother 22: All-Stars in 2020.

Nicole Franzel Expecting First Child With Husband Victor Arroyo

TV celebrity star Nicole Franzel is pregnant.

She and her husband, Victor Arroyo, are expecting their first child, according to people.com. She announced her pregnancy news via Instagram on 10 January 2021, captioning 'Nic + Vic = our Lil' sidekick.' 

She told the outlet:

 "We are so excited to announce that we are PREGNANT! We feel so blessed & grateful."
