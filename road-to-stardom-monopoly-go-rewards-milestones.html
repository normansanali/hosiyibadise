    • How to Earn Points for Monopoly GO Road to Stardom?

    To earn points or tokens in Monopoly GO Road to Stardom and unlock rewards, you have to land on Chance tiles, Community Chest tiles and Railroad tiles. Be sure to aim for those and save your dice multipliers. One thing that we have to mention is that, since there’s a tournament ongoing, landing on Railroads will trigger a Bank Heist or Shutdown, which can earn you extra points. In other words, you’ll have to plan your moves carefully and spend the dice multipliers only when they will net you the most amount of tokens. As you can see in the list above, the amount of points necessary gets insane. Good luck.
