I Can't Stop Wearing These $23 Yoga Pants With Pockets

Flare leggings (aka yoga pants for us millennials) have made a huge comeback this year, and as someone who used to absolutely wear this 2000s fashion staple to death in high school, I’m frankly thrilled to jump back on the trend. My three main criteria for yoga pants still prove necessary in my search for flare leggings today. First off, I need pockets for running errands so I don’t have to bring a purse. Secondly, they can’t be see-through, especially upon bending over (I shudder remembering a pair that once revealed everything). And finally, I ideally want a pair that provides tummy control.

After hours of searching and reading what felt like hundreds of reviews, I finally found an Amazon best-seller that checked off all my boxes: the FireSwan Crossover Flare Leggings. Better yet? These leggings are on sale for only $23, and with over 3,300 five-star ratings, I immediately added them to my cart.

FireSwan Crossover Flare Leggings

These yoga pants have that flare-leg style I was after,  but also come in capri-length and split-leg options. Most of the 19 colors feature a flattering V-waistband, though straight, high-waist styles are also available — both with deep and functional double pockets on both hips. The Spandex-blend material is incredibly stretchy and compressive without being too restrictive, and sizes range from X-Small to XX-Large.

When I first tried these flare leggings, I was honestly surprised at their quality, given how buttery-smooth they felt. The criss-cross waistband cinched in my waist and gave me an incredibly flattering figure. They even got the stamp of approval from my friend who, upon seeing me in the leggings, complimented how great my butt looked. Last but not least, the pockets easily held my iPhone without risk of it falling out, which was helpful for a day of running errands in the city without having to bring a purse. 

I’ve been wearing these leggings for the past month and they’ve held up nicely without stretching out, even after multiple washes. I love how comfy and nonrestrictive these leggings are, so much that I even wore them on a cross-country flight from New York City to California last weekend.I also wore them on a hike and was impressed by how breathable and stretchy the material was.

Hundreds of reviewers shared similar sentiments, with one person raving how “the leggings are so incredibly buttery soft, so flattering, and so easy to wear with anything.” A reviewer confessed they’re “about to buy another pair” now that their first has become their “favorite pants” and a final shopper said the leggings were “100-percent squat-proof.” A number of reviewers were also extremely excited about the pockets, with one stating, “The inclusion of pockets is a game-changer” and a second person claiming that they “love how the pockets are deep.”

If you’re ready to relive the 2000s, grab your own pair for $23 at Amazon while this deal lasts.
