Judd Apatow, Vanessa Bayer, John Early, and More Join Bonnaroos 2016 Comedy Lineup

Today the Bonnaroo Music Festival announced its comedy lineup for 2016, which will take place in Manchester, Tennessee from Thursday, June 9th through Sunday the 12th. Judd Apatow will headline the event, and the lineup also features Adam Devine, John Early, Aparna Nancherla, Bridget Everett, SNL’s Vanessa Bayer and Pete Davidson, and more. Here’s the current lineup:

JUDD APATOW & FRIENDS • ADAM DEVINE • VANESSA BAYER PETE DAVIDSON • BRIDGET EVERETT • PIFF THE MAGIC DRAGON JOHN EARLY • SEAN PATTON • NATE BARGATZE • ALICE WETTERLUND THE GODDAMN COMEDY JAM • APARNA NANCHERLA • BETH STELLING ADAM RAY • JOSH RABINOWITZ • JOYELLE NICOLE • MATTEO LANE JAK KNIGHT • IAN ABRAMSON • SAM JAY

Bonnaroo will announce more comedians over the coming months, so keep an eye on the Bonnaroo comedy lineup page.
