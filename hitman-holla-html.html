Hitman Holla - Age, Family, Bio

College athlete turned battle rapper whose mottos are "Ball Game" and "Yett Yett." He style is notable for being aggressive.

Before Fame

He was born in St. Louis, Missouri and his real name is Gerald Fulton, Jr.

Trivia

He has battled in the leagues SMACK/URL, Battle America and Fight Klub.

Family Life

His father, Big Gerald, is frequently attending his events. His brother, Showout, was involved in a battle rap that went viral on WorldStarHipHop. He and his girlfriend Cinnamon document their life as a couple on their YouTube channel, Hitman Holla & Cinnamon.

Associated With

He has battle rapped against Conceited.
