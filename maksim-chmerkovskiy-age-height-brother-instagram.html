Maksim Chmerkovskiy Age, Height, Brother, Instagram

Maksim Chmerkovskiy is a Ukrainian-American professional dancer and choreographer. Maksim is best known for his appearances on the reality TV show “Dancing with the Stars.”

Aside from “Dancing with the Stars,” Maksim has also appeared on other television shows, including “General Hospital” and “Chasing Life.”

He has worked as a choreographer on the Broadway production of “Burn the Floor” and has choreographed dance routines for various artists and events.

Maksim Chmerkovskiy Age

How old is Maksim Chmerkovskiy? Maksim Chmerkovskiy was born on January 17, 1980, in Odesa, Ukrainian SSR, Soviet Union (now Ukraine).  He is 43 years old.

Maksim Chmerkovskiy Height

How tall is Maksim Chmerkovskiy? Maksim Chmerkovskiy is 6 ft 2 in or 188 cm tall.

Maksim Chmerkovskiy Brother

Who is Maksim Chmerkovskiy’s brother Valentin Chmerkovskiy? Maksim’s brother Valentin is also a dancer.  The brothers formed a successful dance partnership and competed in numerous dance competitions together.

Valentin Chmerkovskiy is a two-time World Latin Dance Champion Junior and Youth and a 14-time U.S. National Latin Dance champion.
