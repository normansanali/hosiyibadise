WWE Raw Results (11/6/23)

In-Ring Segment: WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth “Freakin” Rollins

The champ welcomes the crowd to Monday Night Rollins. Rollins would normally come out here and talk about himself, but he wants to say thank you to two people. The first is Drew McIntyre. McIntyre took him to the limit and because of their battle, Rollins is stronger because of it. McIntyre was a man of his word, he isn’t in league with The Judgement Day. The Judgement Day didn’t get involved in their match. Well, until after. That leads Rollins to the second man he wants to thank. Rollins wants to thank Sami Zayn from saving him from Damian Priest’s MITB cash-in, but he wants to do it in person.

Zayn joins Rollins in the ring. Rollins notes he figured Zayn would show up tonight with Priest’s MITB briefcase. Rollins quips that Zayn must have showed up at the arena, and Adam Pearce made Zayn give it back. Zayn responds that Rollins is 100% correct. Rollins feels he needs to thank Zayn. Zayn stops Rollins and says the reason he stepped in is because he has been fighting The Bloodline for months, and he refuses to allow them to run this show. If Priest wins the world title, it will be Bloodline 2.0. Rollins puts over Zayn for standing up for what he believes in.

Rollins tells Zayn that sometimes, people like him don’t get what they deserve. The Judgement Day doesn’t run Raw, Rollins does. The MITB briefcase isn’t the only way to get a title shot. Rollins went to Adam Pearce and requested a title defense against Zayn. All Zayn has to do is say when. Zayn says when he wins the world title, he doesn’t want to do it against someone who is compromised. Rollins fires up and tells Zayn to cut the crap. Rollins repeats the challenge. Zayn fires up and says he can’t think of a better place than right here tonight. Zayn and Rollins shake hands.

The Judgement Day vs. The New Day

Kingston and Woods take turns working over Priest. Woods lands the Honor Roll. After the break, Priest worked over Woods. Woods tags in Kingston. Kingston clears the ring. Priest floors Kingston. Priest and Bálor set up a double-team move. Kingston escapes. Priest charges in and hits Bálor. Woods lands a diving elbow on Priest. Bálor breaks up the in. Priest gets dumped over the top. Kingston tries a dive but is caught by Priest. Priest hits a complete shot on the commentary desk. Priest drops Woods with South of Heaven. Bálor immediately hit the Coup de Gras for the win.

Winners- The Judgement Day

Shinsuke Nakamura vs. Akira Tozawa w/Alpha Academy

Nakamura lands a strike. Tozawa surprises Nakamura with a German suplex. Nakamura rolls out of the ring. Dive by Tozawa. Nakamura goes up top. Tozawa cuts him off and hits a rana for a near fall. As Gable and Otis cheer him on, Tozawa tries a senton off the rope. Nakamura gets his knees up. Tozawa counters the Kinshasa with a rolls up. Nakamura kicks out and boots Tozawa in the face. Kinshasa by Nakamura. Nakamura pins Tozawa.

Winner- Shinsuke Nakamura

Nakamura mocks Tozawa. Otis gets in the ring and squares up with Nakamura. Nakumara laughs and walks off.

Intercontinental Championship Contenders Fatal Four-Way Match: The Miz vs. Ivar w/Valhalla vs. Bronson Reed vs. Ricochet

As soon as the bell rings a huge brawl breaks out. Ricochet and Miz are left in the ring. Ivar runs over Miz. Miz rolls out. Ivar suplexes Ricochet all over the ring. Ivar hangs Ricochet up on the top rope. Reed flicks the rope, sending Ricochet flying in the air. Ivar catches Ricochet, but Ricochet turns it into a rana. Miz breaks up a dive and almost hits the Skull Crushing Finale. Ricochet escapes. Miz gets whipped into the ropes, but hits a diving dropkick on Ivar. Ricochet tries a dive, but Ivar and Reed catch him and powerbomb him into Miz. Reed and Ivar have a staredown before they both land low crossbody blocks on Ricochet and Miz.

After the break, everyone falls off the top after a tower of doom spot. Miz fires up and clears the ring. Miz sets up his finish on Ricochet. Ricochet turns it into the Recoil. Ivar breaks up Ricochet’s pin with a splash. Ricochet rolls out of the ring. Ivar fires up and lands a flurry of offense. Ivar goes up for a moonsault as Reed goes up for the Tsunami. Miz avoids Reed’s splash as Ivar flattens Ricochet. Miz pins Reed while Ivar pins Ricochet. Ricochet kicks out at 2.9 as Miz gets the three count.

Winner and NEW Number One Contender, The Miz. After the match, Ivar attacks Miz. Moonsault by Ivar.

Earlier today, The Creed Brothers and Ivy Nile sign their Monday Night Raw contracts with Adam Pearce. Tommaso Ciampa and Johnny Gargano walk in and put over the Creeds. #DIY want a match tonight against The Creeds. Adam Pearce makes it official.

The Creed Brothers vs. #DIY

Julius and Ciampa start the match. Ciampa ducks a clothesline as Gargano gets a blind tag. Gargano and Ciampa double-team Julius. Julius lifts Gargano in a deadlift suplex. Gargano escapes. After a series of reversals outside the ring, everyone is down after an apron Brutus Ball. After the break, Ciampa and Gargano hit a back suplex/diving powerbomb combo. Brutus kicks out. Gargano escapes the avalanche Brutus Ball. Ciampa lands the Fairytale Ending. Julius kicks out. While the referee is distracted, Ludwig Kaiser hits the ring and a kicks Gargano in the head. The Creed hits the Avalanche Brutus Ball for the win.

Winners- The Creed Brothers

Backstage, Nia Jax interrupts Becky Lynch’s interview. Jax says no one is throwing her over the top rope tonight. She hasn’t been so happy since the night she broke Lynch’s face. Lynch tells Jax that after Jax broke her face, she went on to glory… while Jax went on to get fired.

Women’s World Championship Battle Royal

Participants: Kayden Carter, Katana Chance, Natalya, Chelsea Green, Piper Niven, Shayna Baszler, Nia Jax, Becky Lynch, Ivy Nile, Zoey Stark, Nikki Cross, Tegan Nox, Indi Hartwell, Raquel Rodriguez, and Maxxine Dupri

As Becky Lynch is making her entrance, Xi Li attacks Lynch from behind. Li drops Lynch with a deadly kick. Referees and officials run down to ringside. Adam Pearce tells Li because of what she just did, she outta here. Pearce tells Li to head to the back. Lynch gets checked on by the medical staff.

Nikki Cross just stands in the middle of the ring. Jax and Rodriguez dump Cross. Tegan Nox and Dupri eliminate Niven. Jax knocks Dupri off the apron to eliminate her while she celebrates. Nile dropkicks Carter while on the apron to eliminate her. Nile press slams Chance off the apron onto Carter to also eliminate Chance. After the break, Rodriguez tosses Green off the apron. Natalya sends out Hartwell. Before Natalya can do anything, Nile kicks her off the apron.

Jax runs over Nile and proceeds to flatten everyone in the ring. Jax knocks Nox off the apron. After a triple team, Jax is tossed over the top. The final four are Nile, Baszler, Stark, and Rodriguez. Before she leaves, Jax pulls Nile off the apron to eliminate her. Baszler and Stark work together to eliminate Rodriguez. Baszler and Stark fight on the apron. Stark ends up locked in the Clutch. Stark escapes and superkicks Baszler. Baszler doesn’t go down. Stark DDTs Baszler on the apron. Baszler falls to the floor.

Winner and NEW Number One Contender, Zoey Stark!

WWE World Heavyweight Championship Match: Seth “Freakin” Rollins (c) vs. Sami Zayn

Rollins and Zayn trade takedowns and strikes. Zayn taps Rollins on the back to remind him that he’s aware it’s Rollins soft spot. Zayn sends Rollins out of the ring. Rollins walks away from a dive by Zayn, but Zayn backflips off the ropes instead. Rollins rolls back in and gets in Zayn’s face. After the break, Rollins and Zayn battle outside the ring, trading chops. Rollins sends Zayn into the barricade. Zayn backflips off the barricade and lands on Rollins. The fight ends up back in the ring, as Zayn works over Rollins with chops. Rollins counters and lays in a few of his own.

Rollins and Zayn trade shots. Rollins floors Zayn with a right hand for a near fall. Rollins lands a dive, but clutches at his back. Zayn responds with a dive of his own. Zayn misses a Helluva Kick. Rollins lands a springboard senton/springboard moonsault combo. Zayn kicks out. After a series of reversals, Zayn back body drops Rollins on the apron. After the break, Zayn hits the Blue Thunder Bomb. Rollins kicks out. Zayn exploder suplexes Rollins into the corner. Rollins counters the Helluva Kick with a superkick. Pedigree by Rollins. Zayn kicks out. Zayn surprises Rollins with a Lion Tamer. Rollins tries to escape, but Zayn turns it into a Boston Crab. Rollins reverses it into a roll up for the win.

Winner and STILL World Heavyweight Champion, Seth Rollins!

After the match, The Judgement Day attacks Zayn on the ramp. Rollins dives through the ropes and lands on everyone. The Judgement Day takes over. Jey Uso’s music hits and he runs to the ring. Jey lands a few superkicks before being attacks by Priest. Cody Rhodes runs down to the ring to even the odds. Referees and officials try to break it up. Adam Pearce grabs a mic and says since everyone wants to play games, they will in Chicago… in WAR GAMES! Everyone starts fighting again.
