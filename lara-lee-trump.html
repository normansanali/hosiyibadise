Lara Lee Trump is an American former television producer, public figure, and model. Lara Lee Trump is famous for marrying Donald Trump’s son, Eric Trump. Moreover, she is a former producer of ‘Inside Edition.’

Early Life

Lara Lee Trump’s birth name is Lara Lea Yunaska. She was born on October 12, 1982. Her hometown is Wilmington, North Carolina, USA. She is 41 years old presently and her sun sign is Libra.

Her father and mother’s names are Robert Luke Yunaska and Linda Ann Sykes respectively. Likewise, she also has a younger brother named Kyle Robert Yunaska.

Moreover, she enrolled in ‘Emsley A. Laney High School.’ From North Carolina State University Lara graduated cum laude with a Bachelor of Arts degree in communication and also studied at the French Culinary Institute in New York.

Career

Lara was a story coordinator and producer for the TV news magazine ‘Inside Edition’ from 2012 to 2016. Also, on March 29, 2021, she joined Fox News as a contributor.

Moreover, Lara is also the producer and host of Trump Productions ‘Real News Update.’ Also, she often thrills her fans by sharing her modeling shots on Instagram.

Lara is a devotee of the full-body fitness program, SoulCycle. Additionally, she supports animal rights and campaigned for wild horses and animals in the animal shelter.

Also, she volunteers for Paw’s Place Animal Rescue, a no-kill, 501(c)3, dog shelter in Winnabow, NC, and Animal Aid USA.

Donald Trump’s presidential campaigns

In Donald Trump’s 2016 presidential campaign, Lara spearheaded the Trump-Pence Women’s Empowerment Tour. And, also served as the Trump Tower liaison for Brad Parscale’s Giles-Parscale company.

After Trump was elected president, she became an online producer and fundraiser for him.

Similarly, in April 2019, she described German Chancellor Angela Merkel’s decision to accept refugees during the European migrant crisis of 2015 as;

“the downfall of Germany; it was one of the worst things that ever happened to Germany.”
