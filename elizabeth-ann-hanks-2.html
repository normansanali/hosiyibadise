Elizabeth Ann Hanks is a writer and has appeared in a few movies. 

She is known as the daughter of Academy Award-winning actor Tom Hanks.

Parents

Elizabeth was born to father Tom Hanks and mother, Samantha Lewes.

Tom Hanks was born on July 9, 1956, on Concord, California, United States.

He is known for his notable work in several movies such as Forest Gump, Apollo 13, Captain Phillips, A Beautiful Day in the Neighborhood, and many more.

Samantha Lewes is an actress and known for her roles in movies such as Mr. Success and Bosom Buddies. 

She was born on November 29, 1952, on San Diego, California, United States.

How Tom Hanks And Samantha Lewes Met

Tom and Samantha met for the first time at Sacramento State University. After that, they started dating. 

They were college sweetheart who was madly in love with each other. 

In 1977, when Tom was a very young age of 21 years, his son Colin Hanks was born. 

At that time, the love birds were unmarried.

But within a year of the birth of their first child, Tom and Samantha got married on 1978.

The couple had two children Colin and Elizabeth Hanks.

But things did not work out, and the couple got separated in 1987. 

Soon after his divorce, Tom married actress Rita Wilson in 1988. 

However, Samantha was not married after then, and according to her son, Colin, shedid not date much and was single till she was alive.

Samantha Lewes Death

In the early 2000s, Samantha was diagnosed with bone cancer. 

After learning that her ex-husband hired a specialist to help her, but as cancer had already spread through her lungs and other parts of the body, there was no cure for it.

She had her last breath on March 12, 2002, in Sacramento, California, USA. 

Her children were left heartbroken by the ultimate demise of their mother. 

Although Tom and Samantha were divorced, they remained good friends, and Tom was devastated by the death of his ex-wife.

Is She Single Or Dating Someone?

Elizabeth has never gone on record talking about her dating life. 

But she has said that she has a good relationship with her ex-boyfriends. 

She has even attended one of her ex-boyfriend’s wedding. 

Several articles has published about Elizabeth being secretly married, but she has not yet confirmed the rumors.

All Children’s Of Tom Hanks

Tom Hanks is father to four children. Colin and Elizabeth Hanks born from his first marriage to Samantha Lewes. 

Truman and Chet Hanks born from his marriage to Rita Wilson. 

Elizabeth is the only daughter of Tom, and Chet is the youngest of all his siblings.

Colin Hanks is the oldest son of Tom Hanks and he is an American actor, producer, and director known for his work in Jumanji, Orange County, and King Kong. 

Colin was married to New York publicist Samantha Bryant in 2010, and they have two daughters together.

Chet Hanks is also an actor and known for his works in Bratz: The Movie, Fantastic Four and Larry Crowne.
