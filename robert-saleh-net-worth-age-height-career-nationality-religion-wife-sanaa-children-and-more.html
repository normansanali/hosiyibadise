Net worth-

Age-

Robert Saleh was born on January 31, 1979. Currently, he is 44 years old.

Height-

He is 6 feet 2 inches long.

Career-

Robert Saleh is the head coach of the New York Jets. Saleh has devoted most of his 20-year coaching career to defense. He held various assistant coaching positions with the San Francisco 49ers, Seattle Seahawks, Jacksonville Jaguars, and Houston Texans between 2005 and 2020.

He served as the defensive coordinator for the 49ers from 2017 to 2020 in his first appointment. Saleh participated in two Super Bowls and won one in 2014 with the Seahawk. At the conclusion of the 2020 season, he resigned from the 49ers and became the Jets’ head coach.

Nationality-

Religion-

Wife-

The couple has seven children – five sons, and two daughters.
