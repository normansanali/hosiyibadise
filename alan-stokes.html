Alan Stokes was born on November 23, 1996, in Hollywood, Florida, USA. Currently, he is 27 years old and his zodiac sign is Sagittarius. He is older than his identical twin, Alex Stokes, by just 2 minutes.

The Stokes twins are half-white and half-Chinese. Alex and Alan currently live in Los Angeles. While growing up, the brothers owned an Alaskan Malamute dog named Sheeba.

Professional Life

Alan and Alex began their social media careers together. They created the channel ‘Stokes Twins,’ which hosts vlogs, life updates, challenges, pranks, and Q&A videos.

Therefore, they launched the channel on April 11, 2008. The twins began working with Brent Rivera in 2017. They are generally known for the short videos that they create on TikTok.

Initially, their YouTube channel was titled ‘alanalan9635’. Soon after they started posting videos on the channel, their charm and sense of humor caught the attention of a lot of viewers.

Q&A videos are the most popular type on the channel. Moreover, they are also part of the YouTube group ‘Sunset Park’.

The Stokes brothers have created several pranks and challenge videos in collaboration with other ‘Sunset Park members. They stopped making videos for about a year, as they did not find any interesting topic.

The Stokes twins have also co-created their music label and website, along with other social media influencers.

The website of the label, ‘heardwell.com’, is a blogging space and an online store that contains their signature merchandise. They attended the 2018 ‘Nickelodeon Kids’ Choice Awards’ along with skateboarder Ben Azelart.

The trio graced the event’s red carpet without wearing shirts under their jackets. The incident became one of the highlights of the event.

The brothers are managed by AMP Studios, United States.

Furthermore, the brothers made their acting debut in 2018. They appeared in the science-fiction TV series ‘Brobot’ and the family TV series ‘Afterschool’. Alex played ‘Lukas’ in both series.
