Eric Bischoff Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Eric Aaron Bischoff (born May 27, 1955) is a television producer, professional wrestling booker, on-screen personality, and occasional wrestler in the United States. He’s also renowned for acting as the Executive Producer and subsequently President of World Championship Wrestling (WCW), as well as the General Manager of the Raw brand of World Wrestling Entertainment. Bischoff has also worked as the Executive Producer of Impact Wrestling for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA). Bischoff, who has an amateur history in taekwondo, has also competed in the ring on occasion and is a former WCW Hardcore Champion. In 2006, he published his autobiography titled Controversy Creates Cash, which was published by WWE Books.
