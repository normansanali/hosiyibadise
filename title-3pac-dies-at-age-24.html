3Pac Dies At Age 24

3Pac died Saturday (October 17) when he was taken off life support after he stopped breathing during water polo practice, AllHipHop reports. The 24-year-old suffered brain damage after being under water for a prolonged period of time.

The comedic rapper was a student at San Jose State University. He published multiple videos on the Internet, including “Rap God (Hardest In The Game Son),” which has more than 600,000 views at press time.

“hardest rapper in the game son,” the video description reads. “eminem not the real rap god son.”
