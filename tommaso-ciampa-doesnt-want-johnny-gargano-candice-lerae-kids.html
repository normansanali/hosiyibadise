Tommaso Ciampa Doesnt Want Johnny Gargano and Candice LeRae To Have Kids

Tommaso Ciampa is quite possibly the biggest heel in pro-wrestling today. The man’s theme song had to be muted at NXT Takeover: New Orleans because it couldn’t be heard amidst the loud boos from the audience.

Needless to say, the former NXT Tag Team Champion always finds new and innovative ways to get heel heat. Recently, he took to his Twitter handle to spoil the ending of Avengers: Infinity Wars to enrage literally everyone that follows him.

Now, Ciampa has decided to take shots at his nemesis Johnny Gargano’s wife, Candice LeRae. Before Ciampa and Gargano became the worst enemies, they were the best of friends and even lived together.

Ciampa shared the house with Gargano and his wife Candice LeRae for nearly two years before he turned on Gargano. He claims that during his time with the couple, he always saw LeRae as the one who “wore the pants in the family”.

However, he went on to say that he was wrong and that LeRae was as pathetic as her husband. He even urged the couple to not have kids in the future.
