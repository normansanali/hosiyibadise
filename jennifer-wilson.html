Jennifer Wilson Missing Where Was She Last Seen?

Nearly 20 years have passed since Jennifer Wilson was last spotted in south-central Kansas. People who knew her remained concerned about her absence because no lead was found in the case. 

Jennifer reportedly got into an argument with her roommate on September 1st, 2002, and was reported missing after the incident. 

The argument started at the club Jennifer worked at, and it lasted until they reached their house, which was around south 143rd Street and east 63rd Street, east of Derby. Some suspect that her disappearance may include foul play.

Jennifer’s roommate allegedly left their house following the argument, and when she came back, Jennifer was not there. 

Her vehicle and beloved dog were among the possessions she had left. Jennifer has a history of performing as an exotic dancer. 

She has been formally pronounced dead because of the suspicion that she could have been the subject of murder given the circumstances surrounding her missing.

The FBI Violent Criminal Apprehension Program is a system that monitors instances of missing individuals across the country. It focuses on Jennifer’s case. 

The only two missing people from Kansans out of the 73 incidents that are currently posted on the website are Jennifer and Adam Herrman.
