Kyle was born on July 12, 1994, in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada. He is the son of Rick and Gayle Forgeard, and has a sister named Chantal. YouTube Career and NELK

Forgeard created his YouTube channel, NelkFilmz, in 2010, along with his former partners Niko Martinovic, Marko Martinovic, and Lucas Gasparini. They started by uploading short skits and funny videos, but later switched to prank videos and vlogs. In 2014, Kyle met Jesse Sebastiani at an MTV Canada conference, and invited him to join Nelk.

Together, they turned Nelk into one of the most popular prank channels on YouTube, with over 7.5 million subscribers and 1.3 billion views as of December 2023. Some of their most viral videos include Coke Prank on Cops, Fake Employee Prank at McDonalds, and Fake Professor on the First Day of College.

NELK, which stands for No One Ever Listens to Kyle, is not just a YouTube channel, but also an entertainment company and a lifestyle brand. The group, which consists of Kyle, Jesse, Steve Deleonardis, Salim Sirur, and Lucas Gasparini, is also known as the Nelk Boys or the Full Senders. They have a loyal fan base that follows their motto of “Full Send”, which means to do something without hesitation or fear of consequences. They also have a premium membership service called Send Club, where fans can access exclusive content, giveaways, and discounts.
