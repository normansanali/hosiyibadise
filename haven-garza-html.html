Haven Garza - Age, Family, Bio

Web star whose garzacrew TikTok account has accumulated 4.6 million fans. She is known for dancing with her twin sister and for posting comedic situational skits. Her mother is often involved in her videos and plays a big part in the account. 

Before Fame

Her mother began uploading videos to her TikTok in April of 2020. 

Trivia

Her garzacrew Instagram features family portraits and reposts from TikTok. One of their most popular TikToks features a time jump in which they age several years. It has gained over 3 million views. 

Family Life

She is the daughter of Adrea Garza. Her twin sister's name is Koti. 

Associated With

On April 2, 2020, she and her twin sister danced to "Savage" by Megan Thee Stallion.
