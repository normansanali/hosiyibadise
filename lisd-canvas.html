About LISD Canvas

  • Canvas LISD is a web-based learning management system (LMS) used by LISD for online teaching and learning. LISD Canvas enables the LISD faculty to use web-based collaborative and assessment technologies to enhance the teaching and learning experience.
  • LISD Canvas includes a variety of customizable course creation and management tools, course and user analytics and statistics, and internal communication tools.
  • LISD Canvas is a web-based software that allows institutions to manage digital learning,
  • Although, Canvas is primarily a web-based software, any user can access Canvas on a mobile device from the Canvas Teacher, Canvas Student, and Canvas Parent apps. Learn more about the Canvas mobile apps.

Uses Of LISD Canvas

  • With LISD Canvas, educators can create and present online learning materials and assess student learning online, as well as allow students to engage in courses and receive feedback about skill development and learning achievement.
  • LISD Canvas allows teaching staff to build and add tools to their course site to more specifically meet their particular pedagogical needs, and administration can build and add tools specific to LISD systems.
  • Instructors can use this service to design full-featured online course environments for participants of the classes. Students can also access course materials, collaborate with others, engage with interactive assessments and assignments, and track their progress in a course gradebook.
  • It is used by learning institutions, educators, and students of LISD to access and manage online course learning materials and communicate about skill development and learning achievement.

LISD Canvas Login Requirements

  • You will be able to access LISD Canvas from any computer, tablet, or mobile device with internet connectivity.

How To Access LISD Canvas

LISD Canvas Login

  • First visit the LISD canvas login portal at: https://lisdtx.instructure.com/
  • Then enter your username and password
  • Then click on the login button
  • Your LISD Canvas dashboard will be opened
  • Use the menus on the page to access a feature, for instance you can Click on ‘Courses’ or ‘My Courses’ to view all courses assigned to your class

    • LISD Canvas App

    LISD Canvas Student App

    • To access the LISD Canvas Student App, first visit Google PlayStore or Apple Store to download and install the Canvas App.
    • Afterwards open the app and tap on “Find my school”
    • Then type the name of your school (ie LISD)
    • A list of schools will be displayed, then tap LISD
    • The LISD canvas login page will be displayed
    • Then enter your username and password to sign into your LISD canvas
