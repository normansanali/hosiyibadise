Who Is Joel Kleefisch? Details To Know About Him

Joel Kleefisch is a former state representative of Wisconsin state and an active politician.

He served for fourteen years in the Wisconsin State Assembly from January 2005 to January 2019.

Moreover, he was elected to the Wisconsin State Assembly in 2004 and was re-elected consecutively for 14 years.

While in assembly, he represented parts of Waukesha, Jefferson, and eastern Dane counties.

Before joining politics, he worked as a news researcher for KCBS-TV in Los Angeles during the summer of 1992.

Also, he was hired by WISN-TV to work at the assignment desk and as a part-time reporter in 1994.

Among his remarkable works, he co-sponsored legislation to increase the penalties in Wisconsin for driving under the influence in 2009.
