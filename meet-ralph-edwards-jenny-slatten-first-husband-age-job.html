Meet Ralph Edwards, “90 Day Fiance” Star Jenny Slatten First Husband

In September 2022, Intouch Weekly revealed that 90 Day Fiance star Jenny Slatten’s first husband/ex-husband is Ralph Edwards. According to Distractify, it is unclear how the exes met each other.

Since then, Jenny has moved on and found love again with her second husband Sumit Singh. Hailing from different parts of the world, they met each other online. Sumit catfished Jenny in 2012 pretending to be a 25-year-old British man named Michael Jones. Later, Sumit came clean with his online persona and they began dating each other eventually.

They met each other in person for the first time when Jenny arrived in India in 2013. She stayed for several months and their bond became even more potent.

There was no surprise, that Sumit’s parents wouldn’t let their son marry an older woman. In addition, Sumit had another secret, his ex-wife who demanded a $500 payment every month and a $20,000 initial payment to ink the divorce paper.

After several trials and tribulations, Jenny and Sumit did get married and Sumit’s mom accepted Jenny as her daughter-in-law.
