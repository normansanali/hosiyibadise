Kathleen Hixson - Age, Family, Bio

Point-of-view style lip sync performer and dancer who rose to fame through the use of her self titled TikTok account. Her videos have received more than 30 million likes on the platform. Her YouTube channel has featured multiple challenge videos like her "Big VS Medium VS Small Food Challenge!!!" video. 

Before Fame

Her oldest archived TikTok was set to Billie Eilish's smash hit "Bad Guy."

Trivia

She created a TikTok duet video featuring Noah Beck. She could be seen with distinctive pink hair on her kathleen.hixson Instagram account. 

Family Life

She wished her little brother a happy birthday in a November 2018 Instagram post. 

Associated With

She frequently collaborated with fellow content creator Alan Stokes.
