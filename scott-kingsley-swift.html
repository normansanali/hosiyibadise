Scott Kingsley Swift is the father of singer Taylor Swift.

Family And Marriage

Scott is the son of Archie Dean Swift Jr. and Rose Baldi (Douglas) Swift.  

He has two siblings. They are Archie Dean Swift and Douglas Swift. 

Scott has two children from his wife, Andrea Swift. They are Taylor Swift and Austin Swift. 

Scott got married to Andrea Gardner Finlay, aka Andrea Swift, on February 20, 1988. 

The wedding took place in Harris Country, Texas. 

Their first child, Taylor, was born in 1989 and second child, Austin was born in 1992. 

The pair got separated in 2014 after being together for 26 years.

Home Invasion Case

As per The Tampa Bay Times, Scott recently fought a burglar who broke into his $4 Million Florida penthouse. 

The incident took place on January 17, when Scott was returning to his home and saw Terrence Hoover coming out of his apartment. 

Reports say that he used an emergency escape stairwell to climb 13 floors to enter it. 

He fought before Hoover ran away. Hoover was later arrested on burglary charges on a $50,000 bond.
