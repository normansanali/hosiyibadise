Eva Longoria and Victoria Beckham Just Had a No-Pants Party in Paris

Pants are apparently optional during Paris Fashion Week this year. First, Hailey Bieber stepped out in her underwear and sheer tights for a night out in the French capital, and now BFFs Eva Longoria and Victoria Beckham are officially hopping on the no-pants bandwagon. 

On Friday, hours after already attending Victoria's spring-summer 2024 fashion show, Eva continued to support her friend at the brand's afterparty in a completely pantsless look. Wearing an oversized lilac double-breasted blazer with a plunging neckline and nothing underneath, Eva ditched her bottoms, except for a pair of nude fishnet stockings. She accessorized with gold platform heels from Santoni, a white handbag with gold chain detailing, and coordinating gold jewelry. Eva's dark hair was slicked back into a sleek, middle-parted bun, and she teamed her smoky eye makeup with a nude lip. 

Beckham wore a similar super-short blazer dress in black, which she paired with see-through patterned tights and black peep-toe platforms. Unlike Eva, however, Victoria wore her brunette hair down in long, loose waves with curtain bangs.

Over on her Instagram Story, Eva shared a snap of the pair posing for photos together outside the party's venue, captioning the shot: "So proud of you always!"

This summer, Eva opened up about the two's unlikely friendship during an interview with The Times. “We are peas in a pod,” Longoria revealed. "I wish people knew how funny and charming and smart Victoria is. She’s the funniest person. I think she’s an introvert, but she’s an extrovert with me. We are inseparable.”

Eva continued, "She’s extremely loyal,” she continued. “There’s never a moment I can’t call her, that she’s not available, that she’s not flying to see me, or I’m flying to see her. Or I’m raiding her closet. She gives me motherly advice and business advice.”
