Liv Tyler Is Pregnant, Expecting Third Child: See Her Baby Bump!

And another! Liv Tyler is pregnant with her third child, which is her second with fiancé David Gardner. The Leftovers actress announced the news via Instagram on Friday, January 8.

“Oh my goodness it seems the stork is visiting us again !!!! I am growing another little Tyler Gardner in my belly,” Tyler, 38, captioned an Instagram pic of her growing baby bump. “Our family is growing!!! So grateful. @davidgardner I love you.”

Gardner also posted a photo of Tyler. “My beautiful baby is pregnant!!!!” he wrote. “Sooooooo excited and so blessed, I love you @misslivalittle.”

As previously reported, Tyler and Gardner — David Beckham’s sports agent — welcomed son Sailor less than a year ago in February 2015.

The actress, who is a daughter of Aerosmith frontman Steven Tyler, is also mom to 11-year-old son Milo, who she welcomed in 2004 with ex-husband Royston Langdon. Gardner has one child with ex-wife Davinia Taylor.

Last year, the Wildling star opened up about motherhood during an interview with Access Hollywood. “I love being pregnant. I’m very tall, so I’m comfortable when I’m pregnant,” she said at the time. “I’ve been very blessed and felt good when I was pregnant. I think also being an actress, it’s sort of the only time I don’t have to work and just be home.”
