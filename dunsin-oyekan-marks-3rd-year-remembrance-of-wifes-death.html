Dunsin Oyekan Marks 3rd-Year Remembrance of Wifes Death

Renowned Gospel artist and worshipper, Dunsin Oyekan, is currently in a pensive mood as he remembers his late wife.

Dunsin Oyekan’s wife Doyin died in 2019 in an unexplained tragedy, and the couple had two children before Doyin’s death.

Dunsin Oyekan took to his Instagram page to commemorate the death of Doyin as today marks 3 years since her demise. 

Dunsin Oyekan recently posted a message on Instagram in her honor.

 “3 Years today… GOD IS GOOD! Oh you loved and served God in me. Yes you did! Keep resting Bybes…”

Elsewhere in Ghana, controversial Ghanaian legal practitioner, Maurice Ampah has bemoaned that the eminent lawyer believes Ghana’s future leaders will be terrible.

Maurice Ampaw disclosed that the anointing for a lot of pastors has reduced exponentially because of the constant viewing of explicit videos and photos on social media.

Maurice Ampaw who has been a stern activist against indecent exposure made this claim during a discussion on Kingdom FM in Accra.

Pastors and young people in Ghana would waste their free time watching pornography online or participating in sex and useless forums, he said, causing their spiritual life and sensitivity to wane.

Maurice Ampaw said; “Many pastors in Ghana view nudes, hence their spiritual life has plummeted.”
