Nikki Bella is an American reality television personality, entrepreneur, actress, model, television host, executive producer, and retired professional wrestler signed to WWE. She is a two-time WWE Divas Champion and holds the record for the longest reign with that title. Moreover, she was ranked number #1 in Pro Wrestling Illustrated‘s “Female 50” list in November 2015 and was named “Diva of the Year” by Rolling Stone in December 2015.

On July 31, 2020, she welcomed a child, a son named Matteo Artemovich Chigvintsev. The couple tied the knot on August 26, 2022, in Paris, France.
